Pre-Review Notes

As part of lecture, we will briefly walk through the following concepts:
  • OS3EP - Devices - Read / Write
Consider the following questions that can be found in the papers directly or reading background materials:
  • Paper 14 - RAID
    • What sequence of events happens on a computer system when doing a read or write from / to persistent storage?
    • What is striping? Mirroring?
    • What is the differences between RAID 0, RAID 1, and RAID 5?
    • How does performance differ between RAID 0, RAID 1, and RAID 5? Think reliability, read / write speed.
    • What is RAID 6?
    • What is RAID 50 / 51?
    • What is JBOD?
  • Paper 15 - DiffRAID
    • How does the failure of a flash drive differ from a spinning disk?
    • What impact does RAID have, specifically, RAID 5 on writes?
Consider the following questions in a group or by yourself (see above):
  • Both Papers
    • How does performance differ between RAID 5 and RAID 6?
    • How would the more common forms of RAID impact file systems such as FFS or LFS?
    • What is the read / write impact of a RAID 50 or 51 approach versus say RAID 5 or RAID 0 or RAID 1?
