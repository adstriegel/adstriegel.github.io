Consider the following questions that can be found in the papers directly or reading background materials:
  • Paper 10 - Fast File System
    • What is the PDP-11? What are its specifications?
    • What is a VAX system?
    • How does the disk speed in the paper compare with modern disk speeds?
    • What are the main components of a "spinning" disk?
    • What is a partition? Superblock? inode?
    • What is a directory? How is it represented in an inode-based file system?
    • What is an indirect pointer? Direct pointer?
    • What are the typical levels of indirection?
    • What is a cylinder group?
    • What is the block size? Why does it matter?
    • What is locality?
    • How many accesses are required to do: cat /home/striegel/hello.txt
    • How does an advisory lock differ from a hard lock?
    • What is symbolic link?
Consider the following questions in a group or by yourself (see above):
  • Paper 10 - Fast File System
