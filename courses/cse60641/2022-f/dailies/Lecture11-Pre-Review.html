Pre-Review Notes

As part of lecture, we will briefly walk through the following concepts:

Consider the following questions that can be found in the papers directly or reading background materials:
  • Paper 13 - Reliability
    • What is a fault? error? failure? How do they differ from one another?
    • What is the purpose of redundancy? How might it differ in hardware and software variations?
    • What is error detection? error correction?
    • What is reliability?
    • What is MTTF? MTBF? MTRF? How are they related?
    • What is TMR?
Consider the following questions in a group or by yourself (see above):
  • Paper 13 - Reliability
    • Does a fault always result in an error?
    • Is there an error always before a failure?
    • What is the relationship between the number of components and reliability? What aspects might change that relationship?
    • How is availability different from reliability? Could you think of an example?
    • How might pro-active versus reactive fault tolerance differ?
    • Are systems that mask faults necessarily less reliable than systems that actively detect faults?
